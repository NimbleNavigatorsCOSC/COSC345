Emulator ideas

The emulator will have a 320 by 320 pixel screen to mirror the size of the smart watch. Clicking on the screen will be like touching it on the watch. Each time the emulator is 'touched' it will send the location of the touch (x and y coordinates) to the app. The app will manage what to do with this information. The emulator will get information about the time from the browser and send it to the app. The emulator will receive information from the app telling it what kind of graphical information to display and what sounds to play.
The emulator will feature two buttons one will simply exit out of the app returning to the 'home page' of the smart watch. The other could be used to perform the same function as some of the touch commmands such as stopping and starting the stopwatch aspect or saving and exiting when setting the alarm.
